What is Ridgeway Cover Manager?

Ridgeway Cover Manager allows staff members at The Ridgeway School to easily and efficiently request lesson cover and room changes – these requests are passed to the appropriate Head of Faculty for approval, before being sent to Cover Management Staff for logging onto the SIMS system.
